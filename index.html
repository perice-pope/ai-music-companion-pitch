What it does

Practice with a teacher in your ear.

Three things Musa does better than anything else on your desk.

Listens like a teacher

Sub-25 ms analysis of pitch, rhythm, articulation, and tone. Musa hears every note you play — no wires, no editing, no waiting.

Speaks your instrument's language

From trumpet articulation to vocal vibrato to violin intonation — Musa's instrument profiles know what "good" sounds like on your horn, voice, or bow.

Adapts to your progress

Your practice dashboard tracks what's improving, what's plateauing, and what to work on next — so every session builds on the last.

"The platform that wins music education will be the one that does what a great private teacher does: listens, understands, remembers, adapts, and speaks the student's musical language."
